**超深海REY泥揚鉱・スマートライザー輸送遷移理論**

## Scope

A10-HSR / HSR v3.3 is a reduced-order pre-engineering transport-transition theory for ultra-deep rare-earth-element and yttrium (REY) mud lifting. It distinguishes sustained transport, blackout/coastdown-induced collapse, and deposition-limited long-duration constraints in a reduced 1.5D fluid-structure / closure-backed surrogate.

The objective is not to provide an immediately deployable deep-sea mining system, certified offshore equipment, or final field-operation procedure.

## Central interpretation

HSR v3.3 should be interpreted as a **practically connectable pre-engineering theory**. It gives a reduced operating-window and diagnostic logic for transport phase transitions:

- sustained-transport sector,
- fault-induced collapse sector,
- deposition-limited horizon.
